Penne Turkey Marinara

Description

  • Time: Approximately 2 hours
  • Calories: 500 - 700
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 200 grams of penne
  • 2 pounds of ground turkey
  • 2 cans of San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1/2 of a medium yellow onion
  • 3 tablespoons of fresh grated parmesan cheese
  • 8 cloves of garlic
  • 1/3 cup of fresh basil leaves

Steps

Marinara Sauce

  1. Use your hands to crush tomatoes with sauce in a large bowl. Set aside.
  2. Dice onion and mince garlic.
  3. Heat a large pot on medium-low flame. Add olive oil and diced onion with pinch of salt. Cook for about 10 minutes until onions are sauted.
  4. Add minced garlic and stir for about 1 minute.
  5. Add tomatoes and sauce to pot and stir.
  6. Add basil and bring to gentle simmer.
  7. Let the sauce simmer for 1 to 2 hours with the lid partially covered. Let cook until sauce has reduced to your liking.

Pasta and Turkey

  1. Place ground turkey in a large pan on medium heat.
  2. Crush with wooden spatula until turkey has cooked into small golden-brown clusters.
  3. Cook pasta in a separate pot according to label's instructions.
  4. Add sauce, turkey, and pasta to one bowl and serve with grated parmesan.
