BBQ Pork Ribs

Description

  • Time: Approximately 5 hours
  • Calories: 650 - 800
  • Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 1/2 rack of any style pork ribs
  • 2 cups of barbecue sauce
  • 2 tablespoons of meat seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons of freshly ground pepper

Steps

  1. Remove fat membranes from ribs with the help of a paper towel.
  2. Pat dry the ribs.
  3. Generously season both sides of meat with ground pepper and meat seasoning. Rub into both sides.
  4. Place on pan and cover with foil
  5. Cook in oven at 275 degrees Fahrenheit for 3 to 4 hours or until tender
  6. Take ribs out and drain liquid. Test that ribs are done by lifting the rack with tongs. If the skin begins to tear, then the ribs are ready!
  7. Rub BBQ sauce onto both sides and place the ribs back in the oven on broil for 2 minutes to crisp crust
  8. Rest for 15 minutes and enjoy!
